How to Read This Calendar

Each card represents one day in the retention campaign. Use the color bands to quickly see the focus of the day, then skim the activity text for execution details.

Activity Types

  • Social Media
    Reels, feed posts, memes, and behind-the-scenes content that drive awareness and shares.
  • PR / Media / Outreach
    Flyers, local press, collaborations, and partnerships that expand reach beyond followers.
  • Email Campaigns
    Follow-up flows, win-back messages, and promos to convert first-time visitors into regulars.
  • Event Days
    In-venue experiences, tournaments, and themed nights that turn one-off visits into traditions.
  • Major Events / Deadlines
    Key milestones such as league launch, finals, and major drops.
